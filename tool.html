Image Optimization Tips

For Web Images

Use JPEG format with 70-80% quality for photos. For web, resize images to the exact dimensions they'll be displayed.

For Print

Maintain higher resolution (300 DPI) and quality (90%+) when preparing images for print.

For Social Media

Each platform has ideal dimensions. Instagram: 1080×1080px (square), Twitter: 1200×675px (landscape).
